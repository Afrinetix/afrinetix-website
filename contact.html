Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens on a free strategy call?+
The 30-minute session is focused entirely on your business. We'll ask about your goals, current challenges, and digital setup. By the end, you'll have a clear view of your top growth opportunities — with or without Afrinetix.
How quickly do you start after we engage?+
For most services, we launch within 1-2 weeks of signing. We have a streamlined onboarding process that gets us from contract to execution extremely quickly.
Do you work with businesses outside Kenya?+
Absolutely. We work with clients across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and beyond. All our services can be delivered 100% remotely, and we're well-versed in market nuances across East Africa.
What is the minimum engagement period?+
For ongoing services (marketing retainers, automation support), we require a 3-month minimum. Project-based work (web development, one-off training) has no minimum commitment.
Can you work within a tight budget?+
Yes. Our Starter packages are designed for growing businesses with lean budgets. We'll always be transparent about what's achievable at your budget level and won't overpromise.
How do you report on results?+
All clients receive real-time access to a live performance dashboard, weekly email summaries, and a formal monthly strategy report. You'll never be in the dark about your campaign performance.
Do you offer guarantees?+
We don't make blanket guarantees (no reputable agency should). However, we commit to clear, measurable KPIs in every engagement contract — and we've never missed a target in our history of engagements.
What industries do you specialize in?+
We have deep expertise in Finance & Fintech, Healthcare, Real Estate, Education & EdTech, and SaaS/Tech. We've also worked with retail, hospitality, and non-profits. If you're in a different sector, reach out — we may still be the right fit.
