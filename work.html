How We Cut NexaBank's Customer Acquisition Cost by 40%

NexaBank · Financial Services · Nairobi, Kenya
The Challenge

NexaBank was spending over KES 1,200 per qualified lead across their digital channels — too high to maintain profitability in Kenya's competitive banking market. Their campaigns were scattered, their SEO non-existent, and their website was converting at just 0.8%.

Our Approach

We rebuilt their entire digital acquisition stack from the ground up. Starting with a deep audit, we identified that 80% of their ad spend was going to the wrong audience. We restructured their Meta campaigns, rebuilt their Google Ads account, and launched a targeted SEO programme focused on high-intent, product-specific keywords.
