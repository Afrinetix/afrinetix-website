Who We Are

The Growth Engine Africa Deserves

Afrinetix Solutions Limited was founded in Nairobi with a bold conviction: African businesses deserve the same world-class digital infrastructure and strategic thinking that powers the fastest-growing companies globally — built for local realities, not imported templates.

What started as a boutique digital consultancy has grown into a full-spectrum AI-powered growth agency. Today, we partner with 20+ organizations across finance, healthcare, real estate, education, and SaaS — delivering measurable transformation through marketing, automation, development, and training.

Every decision we make is rooted in data. Every strategy we build is tied to a KPI. And every engagement we take on, we treat as if it's our own business on the line. That's not a tagline — it's how we operate.
